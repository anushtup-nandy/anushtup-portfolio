Bio-Inspired Fish Robot

Exploring Biomimicry in Robotics

This project focuses on the development of a bio-inspired robotic fish, mimicking the natural movements and behaviors of real fish. The goal is to leverage biomimicry to create more efficient and versatile underwater robots.

Here's some more text about the project. We experimented with various materials and mechanisms to replicate the swimming motion of fish.
